Programs In The Department Of Software Engineering

Our Programs

We offer a wide range of software engineering programs to suit your career goals.

Undergraduate Degree

Our undergraduate program provides students with a solid foundation in software engineering principles, practices, and tools. Students learn how to design, develop, and maintain software systems using industry-standard technologies and methodologies.

Graduate Degree

Our graduate program is designed for students who want to advance their careers in software engineering or pursue research in the field. Students can choose from several specializations, including software design, software testing, software project management, and software engineering education.

Certificate Programs

Our certificate programs provide students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in specific areas of software engineering. We offer certificates in software design, software testing, software project management, and software engineering education.
